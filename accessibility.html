Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Will's Fence & Deck wants everyone to be able to use this website comfortably, including people who rely on assistive technology.

Our Goal

We aim for this site to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. The site is built with semantic headings, descriptive alt text on images, keyboard-friendly navigation, readable contrast, and support for reduced-motion preferences.

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and some areas may not yet be perfect. We review the site as it grows and fix issues as we find them or as they are reported.

Hit a Barrier?

If any part of this site is hard for you to use, or you need information in another format, call us at (903) 749-3828 and we will get you what you need another way. We take these reports seriously and use them to improve the site.
