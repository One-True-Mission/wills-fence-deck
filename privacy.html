Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Will's Fence & Deck ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. It is written in plain language on purpose.

What We Collect

When you submit our estimate request form, we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, email address, city, and the details of your project. We do not collect payment information through this website.

How We Use It

  • To respond to your estimate request and communicate about your project
  • To schedule site visits and work
  • To keep basic records of inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

Third-Party Processors

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, which transmits your submission to us by email. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Both services may process technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of normal operation. We do not run advertising trackers on this site.

Your Rights Under Texas Law

Under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act, you may have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, correct inaccuracies, request deletion, and opt out of certain uses. To make any of these requests, call us at (903) 749-3828 or send a message through our contact form. We will respond as promptly as we can.

Data Retention

We keep inquiry information only as long as needed to serve you and maintain reasonable business records. You may request deletion at any time.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Call (903) 749-3828 or write to us at 122 County Rd 3521, Bullard, TX 75757.

This policy is a plain-language starting point, not legal advice. Business owners should have an attorney review privacy and data clauses if certainty is required.
