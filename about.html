A Local Builder You Can Count On

Will's Fence & Deck is an owner-operated business working out of Bullard and serving Tyler and the surrounding East Texas area. Will works local to provide fencing and outdoor building for homeowners and businesses, and takes real pride in every project he takes on.

We take great pride in the experience, expertise, quality, and customer service we provide to meet each customer's needs. It is our mission to provide excellent workmanship and complete customer satisfaction from the start of a project through to completion.

To understand the needs and expectations of our customers, we take great care to work and communicate with everyone in a professional manner. Our reputation is based on service, safety, and quality, no matter how large or small the job.
