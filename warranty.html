Workmanship Warranty

Last updated: July 5, 2026

We stand behind our work. Excellent workmanship and results built to last are the promise behind every Will's Fence & Deck project, and this page explains what that means in practice.

What Is Covered

Will's Fence & Deck warrants its workmanship on the projects we build, including wood fences, decks and porches, chain link, aluminum and steel, and barbed wire and ranch fencing. If a defect in our installation work appears within the warranty period stated on your written estimate or invoice, we will repair it at no charge. This covers issues caused by how the project was built, such as posts or fasteners not installed properly.

What Is Not Covered

  • Normal weathering of wood, including graying, minor checking, warping, and knot movement
  • Damage from storms, floods, fallen trees or limbs, vehicles, or other events outside our control
  • Damage caused by alterations or repairs performed by others
  • Ground movement, shifting soil, or drainage conditions beyond normal expectations
  • Materials covered instead by a manufacturer's warranty, which we will help you pursue

Making a Warranty Claim

Call or message Will's Fence & Deck at (903) 749-3828, describe the issue, and send photos if you can. We will come take a look and make it right if the issue falls under this warranty.

Liability

Our responsibility for any project is limited to repairing or correcting our own workmanship as described above. We carry out every job with care and coordinate utility line marking where required before digging post holes.

These warranty terms are a plain-language summary. The written estimate or agreement for your specific project controls if there is any difference between the two. This page is not legal advice.
